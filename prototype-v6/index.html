Changing Your Workflow to Cope with Responsive Design

Published by Dan Rose on 08 July, 2015

Responsive design has brought many changes to the web design business, but one sneaky change that has perhaps been overlooked is that designing for so many screen sizes alters the way designers hand off the projects to developers.

That's just one example of a workflow issues that Dan Rose is determined to fix. But since we're talking about how projects are handed back and forth on a team, any fix is going to require a full team effort. Turns out getting everyone on a team who is used to one way of doing things to change is a tricky affair.
